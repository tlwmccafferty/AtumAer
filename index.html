Executive Snapshot

What we do

  • Premium drone shows (100 → 1,000 units) with per‑drone pricing and premium attach (pyro, FPV, rush creative).
  • Education & training cohorts; rentals; a software playground for interactive autonomy.
  • Roadmap to residencies, leagues, and a world‑record attempt via partners.

Operating moat

Vendor‑agnostic acceptance gate (PX4 1.13.x, ≥20‑min endurance, ≥900‑lm LEDs, 10 m/s horizontal, dual‑band comms with ≥400 m / ≥1,200 m, vibration < 0.04, remote sleep/wake or power, fast‑landing tolerance).

How this microsite is built

Today’s MVP (static)

This site is pure HTML/CSS/JS—no backend—so it can be hosted on GitHub Pages, Netlify, or Vercel in minutes. Interactive pieces (timeline & donut) use tiny vanilla‑JS helpers.
